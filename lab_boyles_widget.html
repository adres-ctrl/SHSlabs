Boyle's Law — Closed System
In a closed system, gas cannot escape. When volume changes, pressure changes inversely. Adjust the volume slider and observe what happens to pressure inside the container.
P₁ × V₁ = P₂ × V₂

Part 2
Open System — The Lungs
The lungs are an open system — air flows freely between the lungs and the atmosphere. When lung volume changes, pressure shifts above or below atmospheric pressure (Patm), and air flows along that gradient. The key reference point is always Patm = 0.
